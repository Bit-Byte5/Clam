# Clam

Play Geometry Dash with friends on the same Wi‑Fi.

**Host** a session, enter a main campaign level (1–22), and friends can find you under **Nearby players** and join with one tap. You'll see each other as ghost avatars moving through the level — everyone still plays on their own screen with their own physics.

LAN only. No room codes yet — anyone on your network can join if they have Clam installed.

**Settings:** WebSocket port, discovery port, and whether to show your GD username on LAN.
